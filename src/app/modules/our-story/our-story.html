Our Story

Since day one

Some stories start with a glance. Others start with a word, a laugh, or a perfect coincidence that doesn't feel like coincidence at all.

Ours started the way all the best ones do — unexpectedly, quietly, and then all at once. I didn't plan on you, Carol. But somehow you became the best thing I never planned for.

You have this way of making ordinary moments feel extraordinary. A coffee in the morning, a walk in the evening, a movie we've both seen a hundred times — everything is better with you beside me.

This little corner of the internet exists for one reason only: to celebrate you, us, and every beautiful moment we've shared.
