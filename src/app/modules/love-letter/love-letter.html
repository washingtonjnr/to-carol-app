From the heart

A Letter to Carol

✦

My dearest Carol,

I'm not always the best with words — you know this about me. But some things deserve to be said clearly, simply, and without hesitation:

You make my world brighter. Not in some grand, cinematic way — in the quiet, everyday way that actually matters. In the way I look forward to talking to you at the end of a long day. In the way I laugh differently when you're around. In the way you make even the boring parts of life feel like something worth showing up for.

I love the way your mind works, the way you care, the way you love. I love the version of myself I get to be when I'm with you.

This little site is my small, imperfect attempt to show you what you mean to me. It doesn't come close. But nothing ever really could.

Thank you for being you. Thank you for being mine.

All my love,
always and always
